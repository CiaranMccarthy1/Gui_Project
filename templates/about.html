About the Company

Founded in Galway by Ciaran McCarthy, Kevin Mongey, Dara Maolain, and Richard McGrath our mission is to simplify and enhance the food ordering experience.

This application was developed as part of our final year GUI project, reflecting our commitment to integrating technology with a passion for exceptional cuisine.

From burgers to milkshakes, we have designed an intuitive and user-friendly platform that enables customers to easily order their preferred items with minimal effort.

We appreciate your support of local talent and encourage you to explore FoodApp!
